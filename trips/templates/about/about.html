About Me

I find comfort in nature and joy in culture, history and people. Through my hobbyist photographic journey I try to convey stories and impressions.

Born and raised in Athens and currently living in Amsterdam. I am an engineer by profession, specialising in remote sensing and IoT for a more sustainable world. I bought my first DSLR camera when I was 20 and since then I have been charmed by practicing photography as a hobby, during my free time and my travels.
